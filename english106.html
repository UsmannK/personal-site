Causes

The Biggest Causes

According to the CDC, "stress can have a significant effect on an adolescent's long-term physical and mental well-being." Studies show that the top causes of stress among adolescents are schoolwork, parents, friends' problems, and relationship issues.

Learn More

Causes amongst adults

In contrast, according to NPR the most common causes of stress amongst adults are hearing about politics, wathcing the news, and family schedules. Due to the difference between adult and adolescent causes of stress, teens must make sure to get support tailored to them.

Effects

Of surveyed college-aged students, these were some of the most often reported effects of stress:

Poorer school performance
Feeling irritable/angry
Trouble Sleeping
Turning to alcohol&drugs
