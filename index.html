Brainblaze

A Google Chrome extension that intelligently identifies words that could be replaced by their 'smarter' counterparts/syllables. Across the stack, Brainblaze was written in Python, Java, and Javascript. As a user browses the web, Brainblaze intelligently replaces words with contextual synonyms. Our free extension is made for the 'lazy man/woman.' Once installed, the user can continue browsing as they always have; however, now they will be slowly building up the vocabulary as they do.

Technologies/Concepts used

Javascript, Python, Java, Heroku, Natural Language Processing
